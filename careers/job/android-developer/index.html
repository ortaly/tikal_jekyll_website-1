Android Developer

As an Android Developer at Tikal you will have the opportunity to work on cutting edge technologies in the mobile space, learn from a wide array of frameworks, and contribute to different open source projects. Tikal's unique technical roadmap will take you to the next level of being an Android expert by consulting with clients, working on mobile initiatives, and contributing to the Android community.

Requirements

  • At least 1-2 years of experience in Android Native applications development - A must.

  • At least 3 years of JAVA development experience.

  • Experience in Mobile development (Windows Phone, Symbian, Blackberry, iOS, etc.) - A plus.

  • Experience PhoneGap - A plus.

  • Knowledge of Open Source technologies.
